Who We Are

All Gods Children Foundation is a community-driven initiative based in Uganda, dedicated to improving the lives of underprivileged children through access to quality education. Founded with a deep passion for helping vulnerable children, the foundation was created in response to the challenges many children face—such as lack of school fees, limited learning materials, and minimal academic support. We believe that every child deserves a fair chance to learn, grow, and succeed, regardless of their background.

Our work focuses on removing barriers to education by providing essential school supplies, academic support, and mentorship. Beyond the classroom, we are committed to nurturing well-rounded individuals by promoting personal development, confidence, and life skills. We actively engage with families and communities to raise awareness about the importance of education and to build a supportive environment where children can thrive. Through partnerships, volunteers, and donors, we aim to create sustainable programs that bring lasting change.

This foundation was inspired by the everyday realities faced by many children in our communities—children who are eager to learn but lack the support and resources to stay in school. Seeing this firsthand motivated us to take action and become part of the solution. At All Gods Children Foundation, we are driven by one simple belief: “Every Child, Every Chance.”
