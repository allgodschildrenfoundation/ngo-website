Real Impact: Josh’s Story

Josh is a 10-year-old boy from Hoima who has never been to school. Instead, he spends his days collecting plastic bottles to survive, earning only a small amount that is not enough to change his future.


When we met him, he had one dream — to become a doctor. But without education, that dream is out of reach.


This is exactly why our programs exist — to turn stories like Josh’s into success stories.
