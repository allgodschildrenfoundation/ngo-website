Why Coffee?

At All God’s Children Foundation, we believe that lasting change requires sustainable solutions. Coffee farming is more than agriculture — it is opportunity.

It helps us generate long-term funding to support vulnerable children in education.

25% Directly Supports Education

A portion of coffee initiative income is committed to:

✔ School fees support
✔ Learning materials
✔ Vulnerable family assistance

This ensures long-term sustainability for our mission.
