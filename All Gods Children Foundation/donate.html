Support a Child’s Education

Your donation helps children access school fees, meals, and essential learning materials in Uganda.

Why Your Support Matters

Many children in Uganda struggle to stay in school due to poverty. Your support helps remove barriers like school fees, food, and learning materials.

Every contribution helps a child stay in school and build a better future.

Where Your Donation Goes

💛 $50 — School supplies (books, pens, exercise books, basic materials)

💛 $35 — School meals support for a child (full term support)

💛 $60 — Half school fees support

💛 $145 — Half support package (school fees + meals + school items)

💛 $120 — Full school fees for a vulnerable child

💛 $205 — Full school package (fees + meals + materials)

Every donation directly transforms a child’s education journey.

Transparency & Trust

All donations go directly into supporting children’s education, school needs, and welfare. We are committed to accountability and real impact in every community we serve.
