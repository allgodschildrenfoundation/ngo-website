Coffee for Education

Many people see a coffee plantation.

We see school fees. We see opportunity. We see hope.

Why Coffee?

At All Gods Children Foundation, we believe that lasting impact requires sustainable thinking. Alongside donations and partnerships, we are developing local initiatives that help strengthen long-term support for vulnerable children and families. Coffee farming represents more than agriculture. It reflects our belief that communities become stronger when opportunities are created locally and built responsibly.

OUR COMMITMENT

25% Supporting Education

As part of this initiative, up to 25% of future profits generated through coffee activities may be directed toward supporting Foundation programs. These efforts aim to strengthen access to education, support vulnerable families, and contribute toward long-term community impact.
