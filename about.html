Who We Are

All Gods Children Foundation is a community-driven organization based in Uganda, committed to improving the lives of vulnerable and underprivileged children through access to quality education. We were founded out of a deep concern for children who grow up without the basic opportunities needed to succeed in life. In many communities, children face daily struggles such as lack of school fees, limited learning materials, and no academic support at home or in school. We believe that education is not a privilege for a few, but a right for every child. Every child deserves a safe learning environment, supportive guidance, and a real chance to build a better future regardless of their background or financial situation.

What We Do

We identify children in vulnerable situations and support them with access to education and basic learning needs. This includes school fees support, uniforms, books, and learning materials. We also run after-school tutoring, mentorship, and life skills programs to help children stay in school and perform better academically. Beyond education, we engage families and communities to raise awareness about the importance of keeping children in school and creating long-term change.

Where Your Donation Goes

Every donation directly supports children in need. Your support helps us pay school fees for children who cannot afford education, provide uniforms and shoes, supply books and learning materials, and support tutoring programs. A portion of donations also goes toward identifying new children in need and maintaining ongoing community outreach programs. We ensure that every contribution makes a real, measurable difference in a child’s life.
