Support a Child Today

Your support helps vulnerable children in Uganda stay in school, receive learning materials, and build a better future.

Why Your Support Matters

Many children in Uganda are unable to stay in school due to lack of basic needs such as school fees, uniforms, books, and meals.

Without support, many of them are forced to drop out despite having the desire to learn and succeed.

Your contribution helps us remove these barriers and give children a real chance at education.

Where Your Donation Goes

  • 💛 $5 – School supplies (books, pens, basic materials)
  • 💛 $10 – School meals and daily support
  • 💛 $25 – Full support package for a child

Every donation, big or small, directly supports a child in need.
