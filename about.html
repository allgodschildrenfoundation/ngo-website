About Us

Learn more about All God’s Children Foundation and our mission in Uganda

Who We Are

All God’s Children Foundation is a community-driven organization based in Uganda committed to improving the lives of vulnerable children through access to quality education.

Many children face serious barriers such as lack of school fees, learning materials, and academic support. We exist to ensure that no child is left behind because of poverty.

What We Do

We provide school fees support, uniforms, books, and essential learning materials to children in need.

We also run mentorship, tutoring, and life skills programs to help children succeed both in school and in life.

Where Your Donation Goes

Donations directly support children’s education, school materials, and outreach programs for vulnerable families.

Every contribution helps a child stay in school and move toward a better future.
