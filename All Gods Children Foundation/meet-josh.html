Meet Josh

A real story of a child whose life represents many vulnerable children we support in Uganda.

Josh collecting plastic bottles to survive instead of attending school.

Josh is a 10-year-old boy from Hoima, Uganda.


He has never been to school and works collecting bottles to survive.


His dream is to become a doctor.

Speaking with Josh about his dreams and challenges.

He earns about UGX 150 per kilogram of plastic bottles.

Where Josh sells collected bottles.

Josh needs education, not survival work.
