Who We Are

All Gods Children Foundation is a community-driven organization based in Uganda, committed to improving the lives of vulnerable and underprivileged children through access to quality education.

Many children in our communities face serious challenges such as lack of school fees, no learning materials, and no academic support. We believe every child deserves a fair chance to learn, grow, and succeed no matter their background.

What We Do

We identify children who are out of school or at risk of dropping out and provide them with direct support. This includes school fees assistance, uniforms, books, and learning materials.

We also run tutoring, mentorship, and life skills programs to help children succeed academically and personally.

We work closely with families and communities to encourage education and long-term change.

Where Your Donation Goes

Your donation directly supports children in need.

It helps pay school fees for children who cannot afford education, provides uniforms and shoes, supplies books and learning materials, and supports tutoring programs.

A portion also helps us reach more children in rural areas and expand our programs. Every contribution creates real change.
